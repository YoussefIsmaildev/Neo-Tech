Training Exercises

Follow the exercises below to improve control and performance.

Exercise 1

Hand Open & Close

Improves muscle signal control and basic hand movement coordination.

How to Perform:

  1. Sit in a comfortable position with your arm stable.
  2. Try to slowly open the prosthetic hand.
  3. Close the hand gradually while focusing on muscle signals.
  4. Repeat the exercise 10–15 times.

Exercise 2

Grasp & Release Objects

Enhances grip strength and control over different object types.

How to Perform:

  1. Place a light object in front of you (e.g., a ball or cup).
  2. Try to grasp it using the prosthetic hand.
  3. Lift it slightly, then release it.

Exercise 3

Picking Small Objects

Develops precision and fine motor skills.

How to Perform:

  1. Place small objects like coins or buttons on a table.
  2. Try to pick them up using your fingertips.

Exercise 4

Grip Force Control

Trains the user to control pressure and avoid damaging objects.

Exercise 5

Daily Activities

Helps integrate the prosthetic hand into daily life activities.
